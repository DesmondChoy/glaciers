My career in the investment industry began in 2011 with a portfolio management role in a Fund of Hedge Funds and subsequently with a regional life insurer. In my current capacity, I focus on fundamental bottom-up research on corporate bonds in a regional asset management firm based in Singapore, and I’m a CFA Charterholder since 2014.

Having discovered R in 2018, I very quickly fell in love with the language, packages and community - and have been using data science to augment and refine my investment process. I also collaborate closely with the Investment Technology team to build, train and evaluate machine learning models.

I’m familiar with classical & modern machine learning algorithms (supervised & unsupervised) with a fair understanding of descriptive statistics. I plan on dedicating future blog posts to document my learning journey of applied ML as well as all things data science-related.

This blog is built with blogdown and Hugo, using the Tranquility Peak theme and deployed using Netlify.
