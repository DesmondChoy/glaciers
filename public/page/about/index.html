About

I have been working in the investment industry since 2011, previously with a Fund of Hedge Funds, and subsequently with a regional life insurer. In my current capacity, I primarily perform fundamental bottoms-up research on corporate bonds and contribute to investment ideas in a regional asset management firm based in Singapore.

Having discovered R in 2018, I very quickly fell in love with the language, packages and community - and have been using data science to augment and refine my investment process. I’m familiar with classical & modern machine learning algorithms (Supervised & Unsupervised) with a fair understanding of descriptive statistics - and I plan on dedicating future blog posts to document my learning journey of applied ML as well as all things data science-related.
